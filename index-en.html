Frequently Asked Questions

Is it ethical to create a replica of a deceased person? +

We understand the sensitivity of this question. Our approach is based on informed consent, dignity, and user control. We collaborate with experts to ensure the service is a tool for solace and does no harm, always prioritizing respect for the person's legacy.

What data is needed to create the avatar? +

The more quality data we have, the more accurate the replica will be. This includes voice recordings, videos, emails, text messages, and access to social media. Ideally, the process begins during the person's lifetime through guided interviews. All data is handled with the utmost confidentiality.

Can the digital replica be deleted? +

Absolutely. Heirs or the designated person have full control to pause or permanently delete the simulation and all associated data at any time. We believe in the right to digital oblivion.
